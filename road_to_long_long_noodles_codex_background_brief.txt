============================================================
1. PROJECT IDENTITY
============================================================

Game name:
Road to Long Long Noodles

Core concept:
A slow daily check-in farming game where the player grows wheat over real-world time, slowly builds progress, and eventually works toward opening and operating Long Long Noodles.

The farm is the main screen.
Pawn Shop, Wandering Merchant, and Noodles are separate pages/areas, not crowded into the main farm screen.

Main feeling:
- Calm
- Cute
- Soft pastel
- Cloudy
- Handmade
- Slow progress
- Low stress
- Community-oriented
- Animation-rich
- Detailed art, simple controls

Target players:
Busy young adults and working adults around age 20-40 who like cute, relaxing, daily-progress games. The game can be child-friendly, but it is not mainly designed for children.

The soul of the game:
Community.

Main long-term player pride:
Players should feel proud of farm progress and especially how many noodles they have sold. A Noodles Sold leaderboard should exist later.

Main design principle:
Patience becomes progress.

============================================================
4. FARMING SYSTEM DESIGN
============================================================

Crop:
Wheat only.

Player starts:
- 1 land
- 16 crop slots
- 4 wheat seeds
- 0 gold
- 0 wheat
- no tools
- no Noodles operation

Land:
- 1 land = 16 crop slots
- Normal max later: 16 lands
- Premium lands are on hold and should not be implemented.

Wheat growth:
- Normal official wheat growth: 7 real-life days.
- Growth continues based on real-world timestamps while the player is away.
- No rain system early.
- No pest system early.
- Land starts clean.

Watering:
- After watering, crop becomes thirsty randomly after 18-24 hours.
- If not watered for 36 hours after last watering, crop growth stops.
- If not watered for 48 hours after last watering, crop dries and begins slowly losing growth progress backward.
- For early Version 0.1, a simpler water/growth implementation is acceptable if the prompt asks for basic foundation first.

Weeds:
- Around once per crop cycle/week.
- Weed effect: growth becomes 1.2x slower until removed.
- Weeds do not destroy crops.
- Weed system can wait unless explicitly requested.

Harvest:
- Harvest minigame is future/advanced.
- Early Version 0.1 can use a simple Harvest button if the prompt asks for basic foundation.
- Long-term harvest results:
  - Perfect: 1 wheat + 1 Wheat Fragment
  - Good: 1 wheat
  - Bad: crop growth goes back 1 day
  - Complete fail: crop growth goes back 3 days and may reduce quality
  - Crop is never destroyed by harvest failure
- No seed return from harvest.
- 10 Wheat Fragments = 1 Wheat.

Crop quality:
Future feature.
Starts at 2 stars.
Better care/harvest/tools can improve quality.
Better quality increases chance of 1.1x selling bonus.
Do not implement unless prompted.

============================================================
7. SOUP SYSTEM - FUTURE
============================================================

Future only; not Version 0.1.

Soup:
Bone Broth Soup only.

Ingredients:
- Meat: 500 gold, 5 uses
- Bone: 500 gold, 8 uses
- Magic Seasoning: 500 gold, 12 uses

One soup brew consumes:
- Meat -1 use
- Bone -1 use
- Magic Seasoning -1 use

Brewing:
- Player completes brewing steps.
- Soup becomes ready automatically after 24 real-life hours.
- No manual collect is required.

Soup active duration:
- Active soup lasts 7 x 24 hours after becoming ready.
- One active soup can serve unlimited customers.
- Brewing another soup does not extend the old timer.
- When the new soup becomes ready, it replaces/refreshes the active soup with a new expiration time.

Customer relation:
- Soup is not required for customers to visit.
- Soup is required to serve customers.
- If a customer arrives and there is no ready soup, the player cannot serve that customer.

============================================================
8. CUSTOMER SYSTEM - FUTURE
============================================================

Future only; not Version 0.1.

Customer system:
- Customers are rare Noodles events.
- Each player selects a 12-hour active/awake window.
- Customers are scheduled only inside that selected 12-hour window.
- Customer notification should happen 10 minutes before customer arrival if the system already decided to give the player a customer.
- If player enters within 10 minutes, customer waits up to 1 hour for noodles.
- Customer left: no notification.

Free users:
- 1 customer roll per day.

Premium users:
- Future/on-hold.
- Designed to get 2 customer rolls per day if premium ever launches.

Base customer pity:
- Day 1: 20%
- Day 2: 25%
- Day 3: 30%
- Day 4: 40%
- Day 5+: 50%
Pity affects customer arrival chance only, not Long Long Coin chance.

Customers mainly differ by appearance/animation, not personality.
No reputation system early.
Furniture does not increase waiting time.
